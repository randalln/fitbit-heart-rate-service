# FB Heart Rate Monitor - Privacy Policy

The "FB Heart Rate Monitor" mobile app doesn't collect, transmit, or store any personal 
identifiable information.

## Permissions

* **Internet**: Required to allow the app to receive heart rate data from the Fitbit&trade; watch 
  app, which occurs via a _local_ (loopback) connection (never leaving the mobile device)
* **Bluetooth**: Required to emulate a standard Bluetooth (BLE) heart rate monitor
* **Location**: Required only for Bluetooth negotiation.  The app itself does nothing with this 
  data.
